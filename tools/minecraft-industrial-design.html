Minecraft Industrial Design Project

Project Overview

I have been recreating the Utah State University Logan campus in Minecraft since 2021, working solo to build a detailed, playable replica. To date, about 20 buildings are complete, with careful attention to accurate measurements and color palettes matching Minecraft blocks.

Goal: Showcase progress, invite future contributors, and eventually pitch this as a university club.

  • Precise building replication from Google Maps 3D views.
  • Consistent color palettes mapped to Minecraft blocks.
  • Collaboration encouraged as the project grows.
